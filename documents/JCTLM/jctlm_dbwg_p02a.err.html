<p id="_5a727fa3-65c6-638c-57ef-9d6b7ef51556">Nominations are solicited exclusively from producers of CRMs and developer/owners of RMM/Ps
The Secretariat announces an open solicitation for nominations of CRMs and RMM/Ps for inclusion in the JCTLM database of available materials and methods/procedures of a higher metrological order. The solicitation is made each year from February to the end of May.
