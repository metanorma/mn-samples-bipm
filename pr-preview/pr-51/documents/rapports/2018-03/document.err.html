<title id="_cc5fdf14-ccd6-ae0e-eaa5-eafcdfd96bf5">Results of the comparison</title>
<p id="_34db5967-539a-8dfd-d539-9210528eadde">SRP17, maintained by the CHMI, was compared with the SRPs maintained by the BIPM following the general procedure outlined above. A cycle of twelve comparison runs between SRP27, SRP28 and SRP17 were performed. Ozone was generated using the Environics 6100 generator with airflow of <asciimath>10 "unitsml(L/min)"</asciimath>. The nominal ozone mole fraction range covered during this exercise was <asciimath>0 "unitsml(nmol*mol^(-1))"</asciimath> to <asciimath>500 "unitsml(nmol*mol^(-1))"</asciimath> for the eleven first runs, then <asciimath>0 "unitsml(nmol*mol^(-1))"</asciimath> to <asciimath>840 "unitsml(nmol*mol^(-1))"</asciimath> for the last run. The result of the last comparison run is presented in detail in the <xref target="measurement_results"/>. The repeatability of the results over the twelve runs is shown in <xref target="repeatability"/>, and the stability of the BIPM standards in <xref target="history"/>.</p>
